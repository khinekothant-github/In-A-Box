Recommended Nutritional Information Apps

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular nutrition tracking app that helps users track their calorie intake and monitor their macro and micronutrient consumption. The app features a large database of foods, allowing users to easily log their meals and snacks. MyFitnessPal also provides insights into your daily nutritional intake, and offers personalized suggestions to help you reach your nutritional goals.

Download Now

Lose It!

Lose It! is another nutrition tracking app that allows users to log their meals and track their calorie intake. The app also provides users with personalized nutrition goals based on their weight, height, and lifestyle. Lose It! features a barcode scanner that makes it easy to log packaged foods, and provides insights into your daily nutritional intake.

Download Now

Fooducate

Fooducate is a nutrition app that helps users make healthier food choices by providing them with information about the nutritional content of the food they eat. The app features a barcode scanner that analyzes the nutritional information of packaged foods, and provides users with a letter grade based on the food's nutritional quality. Fooducate also offers personalized nutrition suggestions and recipe recommendations to help users make healthier food choices.
